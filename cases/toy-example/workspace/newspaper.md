## DEATH ON THE NORTH QUAY

The night courier **Silas Dunmore** was found dead at first light on the boards
of the North Quay, struck once from behind. The Harbour Office confirms only that
a ledger in Dunmore's charge is "unaccounted for" and will say no more. The
constabulary are said to favour the obvious view — that the dead man was himself
the smuggler the Office has long sought, and fell out with his confederates.

## HARBOUR OFFICE — NOTICE

The Office reminds masters that the cipher ledger of harbour fees remains the
property of the Office, and that its keeping is entrusted only to bonded staff.
Any person finding Office property is to return it to the night supervisor.
