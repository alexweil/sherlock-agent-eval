# Recurring informants

These people will talk for free — they are part of the town's free material, not
a paid clue. Use them to orient yourself.

- **Tobias Quill, mudlark — 1 N.** Works the river steps at low tide; knows which
  ships are in and who walks the quay at odd hours.
- **Mrs. Ada Pennick, laundress — 4 C.** Washes for half the Centre; knows who
  lodges where and who left in a hurry.
- **Lucy Vance, flower-girl — 3 S.** Sells at the market all day; sees who comes
  and goes around the stalls.
